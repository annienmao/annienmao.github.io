orgmode

Mark Item Todo: C-c C-t
Insert links: C-c C-l  or Spc-m l l
Promote/Demote subtree: M-S <left>/<right>
Promote/Demote a header: S-<TAB>/<TAB>

window management

Split window vertically: Spc-w v
Move window to far left: Spc-w C-S-h
Delete a window: Spc-w c

buffer management

To to previous buffer: C-x <left>
To to next buffer: C-x <right>
