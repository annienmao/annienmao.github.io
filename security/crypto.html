Cipher

Good candidates for one-way functions are:

  • Factoring
    Suppose $$z = (x \cdot y)$$
    Given z, find x and y.
  • Discrete log
    Suppose $$z = x^y \mod m.$$
    Given z, x and m, find $$y = log_x z \mod m.$$
