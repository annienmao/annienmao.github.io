Find ROP chain in code

!mona rop -m "VulnApp1.exe" -cp nonnull

-type: “asc”, “bin”, “ptr”, “instr” and “file”
!mona find -type asc -s "w00t"
!mona find -s "\xff\xe4" -m "VulnApp3.exe"

Use msf-nasm_shell to find the opcode to a specific instruction.
